About Me

Hello, my name is Tyler Crutchley. I am an alumni from Barbara Ingram School for the Arts’ Photography and Cinematography program, as well as a current student at the Sheffield Institute of the Recording Arts in their VideoWorks program. I have a Certification as a Professional in Adobe Premiere Pro, and am currently working towards a certification in Responsive Web Design and Full Stack Development.

As long as I can remember I’ve been a student of the arts. Whether it was singing in the school musical, learning an instrument, acting, or animating, it was always my way of expressing myself. As a student at the Barbara Ingram school for the Arts, I furthered that through courses in Game Development and Animation. However, after realizing that it wasn’t the proper path for me, I looked for something else within video production and landed on Photography and Cinematography.

For two years I heavily focused on developing my skills in live action filmmaking, going from scrappy one man productions, to upwards of a ten person crew (and even some professional PA work). I learned very quick, and now with the experience of directing 5 student short films, producing 4, and writing 3, I feel confident in my abilities in many different areas of post-production, production, and pre-production, having proved time and time again that I can pull off major projects in a tight timeframe with a high level of quality.

If you need someone to work on your next project, I’m your guy! Please reach out to me via the contact form at the bottom of the site, I look forward to hearing from you!
